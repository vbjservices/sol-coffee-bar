About

We already loved coffee, but since going to El Salvador in January our passion for coffee grew.

It was so cool to see the whole process from bean to espresso, and we learned more about tasting coffee.

We got the crazy idea to open our own coffee bar to combine our love for coffee and people.

The coffee bar used to be a restaurant, which Wim and Eva converted with their warmth and love for people and coffee.
